About

Hi! Welcome to McAbe Software.

I'm a mom who enjoys building small iOS apps — mostly the kinds of things I find myself wanting in everyday life. Card games with the family that need a scorekeeper. A simpler way to track my own trades. Little tools that don't exist, or don't exist the way I want them to.

These apps are built for me, by me, but if they're useful to you too, that's the best outcome I could ask for. If you have feature requests or problems, I'm happy to work with you to adjust the features for you!

Thanks for stopping by.
