Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

The short version

McAbe Software apps do not collect any personal data. Everything you do in the apps stays on your device.

What we collect

Nothing. Our apps do not include analytics, tracking, or any form of user data collection. We don't know who you are, how often you use the apps, or what you do in them.

What we store

Any data you create in the apps (game scores, trade history, etc.) is stored locally on your device. It is not transmitted to us or to any third party.

Third-party services

Our apps do not currently use any third-party services that collect user data.

Advertising Coming soon — TBD

We plan to introduce advertising in a future version of our apps. When we do, this policy will be updated to describe the ad network(s) used, what data those networks collect (for example, device identifiers or approximate location for ad relevance), and any choices available to you. Until then, no ads are shown and no ad-related data is collected.

Children's privacy

Our apps are suitable for general audiences. Because we do not collect any personal data, we do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach out at support@mcabesoftware.com.

Changes to this policy

If we change this policy, we'll update the "Last updated" date above. Material changes (like adding advertising) will be clearly called out.
